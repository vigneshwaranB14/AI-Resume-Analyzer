# AI-Resume-Analyzer
AI-powered resume analyser built with Streamlit and Groq (LLaMA 3.3 70B). Upload your resume and get instant feedback tailored to your target job role.
Features

- Upload resume as **PDF or TXT**
- Enter your **target job role** for tailored feedback
- AI-powered analysis using **LLaMA 3.3 70B via Groq**
- Detailed feedback including:
  - Overall impression
  - Strengths
  - Areas of improvement
  - Missing keywords & skills
  - Formatting suggestions
  - Score out of 10
- Fast and completely free to use
